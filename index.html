A popular tourist destination known for its golden sand,clear blue waters, and surf culture.It is the world's most photographed swimming pool, Bondi Icebergs.

Berlin, Germany

Berlin is the capital of Germany and is known for its landmarks, cultural scene, and way of life.

Khwaeng wat arun, thailand

Wat Arun, also known as the Temple of Dawn, is located at 158 Thanon Wang Doem Khwaeng Wat Arun Khet Bangkok Yai Krung Thep Maha Nakhon Thailand 10600.

Rome, Italy

Rome is the capital of Italy and the largest city in the Lazio region. It's located on the Tiber River in the central-western part of the Italian Peninsula.

fuvahmulah, maldives

Fuvahmulah is a small island in the southern Maldives. The island is located on the equator between the Addu and Huvadhoo atolls

Greater London,England.
